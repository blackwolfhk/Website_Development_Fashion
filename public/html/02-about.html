About Us


OUR STROY


Welcome to Free Style, your top pick of techwear and streetwear style clothing. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of techwear and streetwear, with a focus on techwear style, cyberpunk style, urban utility.

Founded by a group of passionate young techwear lovers, Free Style has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for “making more affordable techwear clothing” drove us to study and research the best techwear in the market, then working on making our own techwear. so that Free Style can offer you the most aggressive value of techwear style clothing. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website.

We founded the brand Free Style in 2020, the year COVID-19 started. When we were forced to stay at home, we got a lot of time to think about things other than business work. We generated a group of techwear lovers, talking about what is our ideal techwear, an idea popped up: Why don't we build our own techwear? Then we pushed this idea came true, we did have a techwear brand right now.


MISSION STATEMENT


We called ourselves Free Style because we hope customers and us could be in a more friendly atmosphere like a club or pub. And we don't have physical stores because we still got our own jobs to do. But thanks to that, we don't need to pay for the rent for stores, giving us the opportunity to keep the price in a really decent price range. Price shouldn't be the barrier stopping you or anybody from having a good value techwear style clothing. That is Free Style's mission statement.

After the development of about a year, we do find out that streetwear is pretty close to techwear in some way. Streetwear is a strong way to express your own appeal of your understanding and lifestyle, especially in cloth painting and design. Comparing with techwear, streetwear is more passionate and energetic for young generations. Techwear clothing uses low-key fabrics in black/white/grey to protect users in urban living, while streetwear is using draft painting to go against the world. Two contrasting groups of users surprisingly share similar characteristics. That could be the reason why we are starting to have streetwear collections in Free Style.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
