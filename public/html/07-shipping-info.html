Shipping Process

Please be noted that the usual shipping days of Free Style are Monday / Wednesday / Friday with the exception of holidays (GMT+8).
To ensure the product condition of your order, we will examine the products again before we send out the package. Recently our system detected some scam orders, and it causes a manual check for order situation, which would take less than 5 business days to check your order items. After the order check, your package will be sent and you should be able to check your package information by Order Track. Please contact us if you need to assist due to any urgent situation.


Import Duties

Customs and import duties and fees may be applied to international orders when the shipment reaches its destination country, such extra fees will be charged to the recipient by DHL / FedEx / EMS / SF-Express or your local customs office directly. Free Style has no control over such extra charges. Please note that such fees and charges are the responsibility of the recipient and vary from country to country.
